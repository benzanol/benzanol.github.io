Use arrow keys (or vim keys) and +/- to navigate around the packing.

Use WASD to change each radius ratio (each is between 0 and 1) of the 3 circles generating the packing. The red dot indicates where on the graph this packing lies (the x axis is ratio of the largest radius to the second largest, the y axis is the ratio of the second largest radius to the smallest.)

The dark lines on the graph represent half-plane packings, which occurs when the edges of the circles in the packing align to create a line.

Intersections of two dark lines on the graph represent line packings, which occur when the edges of the circles perfectly align to create two lines, bounding the packing from above and below.
